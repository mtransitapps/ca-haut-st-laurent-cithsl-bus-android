Full description:
This application adds RTM (CIT) Haut-Saint-Laurent bus information to MonTransit.

This app contains the buses schedule and the latest news from @exo_SO_HSL on Twitter.

RTM (CIT) Haut-Saint-Laurent serves Huntington, Ormstown, Godmanchester, Howick, Très-Saint-Sacrement, Sainte-Martine and Mercier.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit app will display bus information (schedule...).

This application only has a temporary icon and no screen: download the MonTransit app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link http://goo.gl/pCk5mV

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from the GTFS file provided by the Agence Métropolitaine de Transport.
https://www.amt.qc.ca/en/about/open-data

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-haut-st-laurent-cithsl-bus-android

This app is not related with the Réseau de Transport Métropolitain (AMT) and RTM (CIT) Haut-Saint-Laurent.

Permissions:
- Other: required for news from Twitter.
